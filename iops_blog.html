    • Is Our Privacy Safe in a Hyper-connected World?

      In our society, technology has progressed at a staggering rate. With this advancement a new form of technology has emerged, called the 'Internet Of Things' (IOT). The IOT is a new breed of technology where everything is connected to the internet and each other. These devices include those used in home automation, entertainment devices, and even light bulbs. CNBC predicts that by 2020 there will be 21 billion connected devices. While the velocity of these devices being developed keeps increasing. It appears that the security behind them has been lacking behind.

    A report from TechRepublic released that 77% of companies believe the IOT has created 'significant' security gaps.
